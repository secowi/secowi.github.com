What Safari School is

Safari School will offer 4-week-long courses of hard travel through an unusual or developing region.

During this travel, participants will engage in Socratic discussions of texts, writing and other assignments, local cultural immersion, and elective activities including volunteering, cooking, games, fitness training and more, aimed at fun and self-development.

Your Role in Safari School

After the initial beta test course, Safari School will enroll participants in professionally guided and taught courses, with the cost of participating in a course as low as $4500 + airfare. Each course will include 10-15 participants, including the Guide and 1-2 Teachers.

The Why That Will Dictate Everything Else

The mission of Safari School courses is radical self-development in knowledge, comprehension, confidence, authenticity, life skills, empathy, fitness, happiness, teamwork, relationship skills and health.

Objective and subjective measurements of change from the course will be conducted by tests completed before and after the course (for example, fitness tests, health metrics, graded writing samples, and statements about important personal relationships).
